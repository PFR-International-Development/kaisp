Kenya Avocado Industry Support Programme

On this site are key documents produced by the Kenya Avocado Industry Support Programme (KAISP).

Delivered by the New Zealand Institute for Plant and Food Research and funded by the New Zealand Government’s Ministry of Foreign Affairs & Trade, KAISP sought to increase economic and food security, and support climate smart agriculture in Kenya with a particular focus on avocado farming systems. The decade-long program had many wonderful collaborators and ended in mid-2023.  Sharing these key documents on this publicly available site is one way the innovation of KAISP can continue to have a positive impact.

Please acknowledge New Zealand Institute for Plant and Food Research (and our KAISP partners where appropriate) if re-publishing any documents on this site.
